Design Principles

Navigation

Navigation should be easy to cover the entire page without going too much into details. The first 15 seconds is how we impress users.

Hierarchy

Keep the hierarchy of the page at a minimum. Focus on "less is more".

Color

Make a subtle color for the background that does not interfere with the main text. Every colored word should have links to other page.
